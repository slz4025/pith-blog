Adding Transclusion

June 21, 2020
Christian Broms

We've recently been working on adding a kind of hyperlinking functionality to the Pith prototype, called transclusion. Often, authors will want to quote a previous post and will cut out a section of it as a reference. This is a behavior we'd like to encourage in Pith, so we're making it easy with a search that's built into the editor.

Transclusion is different than a typical reference in that it includes the original block in the post, rather than a copy of it.
